Our Story

Founded in 2005, A's Computer Store started as a small family-owned business in Georgetown, dedicated to providing high-quality computing solutions to the local community. With a passion for technology and a commitment to customer satisfaction, the store quickly expanded its product offerings and services. Over the years, A's Computer Store has grown into a trusted name in Georgetown's tech industry. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, adapting to the ever-evolving world of computers and electronics. We take pride in our knowledgeable staff, who are not just experts in the latest gadgets but are also dedicated to helping customers find the perfect solution for their needs. From our humble beginnings to becoming a one-stop-shop for laptops, phones, tablets, computers, and comprehensive services, our story is a testament to our dedication to excellence and our deep-rooted connection with the Georgetown community.

Our Mission

At A's Computer Store, our mission is to empower individuals and businesses through quality technology solutions and exceptional services. We are committed to providing a curated selection of top-notch laptops, phones, tablets, and computers, ensuring that our customers have access to cutting-edge and reliable products. Our comprehensive services, including skilled repair and software installation, aim to enhance the functionality and longevity of our customers' devices. We prioritize a customer-centric approach, offering personalized assistance, expert advice, and fostering lasting relationships with our community.
